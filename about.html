About Mannu Bhai Namkeens

Founded in 1990 by Shri Hariram Prajapat in Panchkula, Mannu Bhai Namkeens began with a simple vision: to share authentic Indian snacks crafted with traditional recipes and unmatched quality.

From humble beginnings, our journey expanded to Chandigarh in 2007, where we became a household name known for taste, trust, and quality.

In 2021, a state-of-the-art manufacturing unit was established in Panchkula Industrial Area, blending modern technology with time-honored recipes to deliver premium namkeens at scale.
